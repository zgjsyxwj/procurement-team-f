# Product Overview

CDP Procurement Platform — an enterprise procurement management system covering the full procurement lifecycle: supplier management, RFQ (request for quotation), contract management, and payment processing.

## Domain

The platform connects internal buyers/managers with external suppliers through a unified workflow.

## Roles

- ADMIN (采购经理) — full data access, system management, approvals
- BUYER (采购员) — manages assigned suppliers, RFQs, contracts
- BUSINESS_USER (业务人员) — initiates purchase requests, views progress
- SUPPLIER (供应商) — manages company info, responds to RFQs, views contracts
